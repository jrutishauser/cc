A Waterfront Lifestyle

Ever dreamed of affordable waterfront living?

Plan a day at the beach or explore the other attractions in our Merritt Island community.

Cape Crossing Homes Designed with You in Mind

Cape Crossing homes meet the needs for individuals, couples, and families. We offer three and four bedroom spacious residences, some with configurations conducive to combining units for large families, with modern, stream-lined elements, and amenities that will please all.

Every home will offer thoughtful layouts with abundant light and cross-breezes bringing nature indoors, with balconies where you can sip your morning coffee, with garage parking available for all units.

Kitchens will have custom granite counter tops, designer cabinets, and energy-efficient appliances sure to please your inner chef.

Bathrooms will offer a thermostatic shower system to ease aching muscles, granite vanity tops, tile bath or shower surrounds in all bathrooms, and ceramic tile floors.
